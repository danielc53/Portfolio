Mac Miller

Biography:

Malcolm McCormick, known by his stage name "Mac Miller" was born and raised in Pittsburg, PA. He started releasing his music in highschool and would continue until the days of his passing almost a decade later.

Fun facts about Mac Miller

  • Studio album "Blue Slide Park" was the first independently distributed album to make its way to the top of the US Billboard 200 since 1995.
  • Mac Miller attended Pittsbburg's Taylor Allderdice High School alongside other artists that would rise to stardom around the same time as Mac.
  • Mac Miller released more unofficial mixtapes (13) than he did studio albums (6).
